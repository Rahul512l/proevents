About Us

Founded in 2017, ProEvents & Promotions Pvt. Ltd. has been at the forefront of event management and experiential marketing. Headquartered in Mohali with operations across India and the Middle East, our team leverages decades of combined expertise to conceptualise, design and execute memorable events. Whether it’s a high‑profile conference, a product launch, a vibrant cultural festival or a roadshow, we bring meticulous planning, creativity and attention to detail to every project.
